LEGAL CENTRES AND CLINICS

Legal centres offer information and support of various kinds, particularly to those who are unable to afford a lawyer’s fees.

Legal Aid:

Provides legal representation and access to justice for the poor.
0800 110 110 or www.legal-aid.co.za

Legal Resources Centre:

Provides legal aid for the disadvantaged.
011 836 9831 or www.lrc.org.za

Nicro:

Provides services to adults and children in conflict with the law.
021 462 0017 or www.nicro.org.za

Tshwaranang Legal Advocacy Centre:

Provides free legal services to women.
011 403 4267 or www.tlac.org.za

Women’s Legal Centre:

Provides legal advice via phone or email; aims to advance women’s struggle for equality.
021 424 5660 (CT) or 011 339 1099 (JHB) or www.wlce.co.za
